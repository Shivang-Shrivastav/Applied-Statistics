Do charges of people who smoke differ significantly from the people who don't?

  • This is a two-tailed test.
  • This is a test of comparison of means.

**Ho = The means of CHARGES of SMOKERS and NON SMOKERS are equal.**

**Ha = The means of CHARGES of smokers and NON SMOKERS are significantly different.**

**Here, the sample is greater than 30, so we will use Z-test.**

**Here, we has assume the level of significance as 5%(alpha=5%)**
